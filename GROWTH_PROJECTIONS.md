# A11yScan Growth & Revenue Projections

## Market Context

**Accessibility Testing Market:**
- Global market size: ~$1.2B (2024), growing 25% annually
- Driven by: ADA lawsuits, WCAG 2.1 requirements, EU Accessibility Act (2025)
- Target customers: 
  - 30M+ websites globally
  - 200K+ agencies/consultancies
  - 500K+ development teams
- Average willingness to pay: $100-500/month for automated testing

**Competition:**
- Wave (browser extension) - Free, limited
- axe DevTools - $500-2000/year per user
- Accessibility Insights - Free (Microsoft)
- Lighthouse - Free (Google)
- Enterprise solutions: $10K-50K/year

**Your Advantage:**
- Full-site automated crawling (competitors focus on single pages)
- Professional PDF reports (client-ready)
- Mid-market pricing gap ($99-299/month sweet spot)
- Easy setup vs complex enterprise tools

## What Could Go Wrong üî¥

1. **Too few free ‚Üí paid conversions (<2%)**
   - Solution: Improve onboarding, prove value faster, time-limit free tier

2. **High churn (>8%/month)**
   - Solution: Monthly scheduled scans, better engagement, annual plans

3. **Sales cycle too long (>6 months)**
   - Solution: Target smaller companies, self-serve signup, free trial

4. **Can't acquire customers profitably (CAC > LTV)**
   - Solution: Focus on organic growth, content marketing, reduce paid ads

5. **Free tools are "good enough"**
   - Solution: Emphasize full-site scanning, reports, automation, ongoing monitoring

6. **Enterprise wants on-premise**
   - Solution: Cloud-only until $50K+ MRR, then consider

7. **Legal/compliance issues**
   - Solution: Clear disclaimers, partner with accessibility lawyers, insurance
